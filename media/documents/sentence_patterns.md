| Structure                      | Meaning (বাংলা)                  | Example                                                                                         |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **let alone**                  | তো দূরের কথা                     | I can't afford a bicycle, **let alone** a car. (আমি সাইকেলই কিনতে পারি না, গাড়ি তো দূরের কথা।) |
| **rather than**                | ...এর পরিবর্তে                   | I'd walk **rather than** wait. (অপেক্ষা করার চেয়ে আমি হাঁটব।)                                  |
| **whether...or**               | হোক...বা                         | I'll go **whether** it rains **or** not. (বৃষ্টি হোক বা না হোক আমি যাব।)                        |
| **had better**                 | ...করাই ভালো (শক্তিশালী পরামর্শ) | You **had better** leave now. (তোমার এখনই চলে যাওয়াই ভালো।)                                    |
| **it's high time**             | অনেক আগেই করা উচিত ছিল           | It's **high time** you studied harder. (তোমার আরও মনোযোগ দিয়ে পড়ার সময় অনেক আগেই হয়েছে।)    |
| **no sooner...than**           | না হতেই                          | **No sooner** had I sat down **than** the phone rang. (আমি বসতেই ফোন বেজে উঠল।)                 |
| **hardly...when**              | মাত্র...করতেই                    | **Hardly** had we arrived **when** it started raining.                                          |
| **scarcely...when**            | মাত্র...করতেই                    | **Scarcely** had she entered **when** everyone applauded.                                       |
| **lest**                       | যেন না                           | Work hard **lest** you fail. (পাছে ফেল করো।)                                                    |
| **provided (that)**            | যদি এই শর্তে                     | You can come **provided that** you finish your work.                                            |
| **providing (that)**           | যদি                              | I'll help you **providing** you're honest.                                                      |
| **as long as**                 | যতক্ষণ / যদি                     | You can stay **as long as** you're quiet.                                                       |
| **unless**                     | যদি না                           | You won't pass **unless** you study.                                                            |
| **even if**                    | যদিও / এমনকি যদি                 | I'll go **even if** it rains.                                                                   |
| **even though**                | যদিও                             | **Even though** I was tired, I finished the work.                                               |
| **although / though**          | যদিও                             | **Although** he is young, he is wise.                                                           |
| **in spite of**                | সত্ত্বেও                         | **In spite of** the rain, we played.                                                            |
| **despite**                    | সত্ত্বেও                         | **Despite** being tired, she continued.                                                         |
| **because of**                 | ...এর কারণে                      | The match was canceled **because of** the rain.                                                 |
| **due to**                     | ...এর কারণে (formal)             | The delay was **due to** heavy traffic.                                                         |
| **owing to**                   | ...এর কারণে (formal)             | Flights were delayed **owing to** fog.                                                          |
| **so that**                    | যাতে                             | Study hard **so that** you can succeed.                                                         |
| **in order to**                | করার জন্য                        | I exercise **in order to** stay healthy.                                                        |
| **so as to**                   | করার জন্য (formal)               | He whispered **so as to** avoid waking the baby.                                                |
| **too...to**                   | এত...যে করতে পারে না             | She is **too** young **to** drive.                                                              |
| **enough to**                  | যথেষ্ট...করার জন্য               | He is old **enough to** vote.                                                                   |
| **so...that**                  | এত...যে                          | It was **so** cold **that** we stayed inside.                                                   |
| **such...that**                | এমন...যে                         | It was **such** a good movie **that** everyone cried.                                           |
| **as...as**                    | যতটা...ততটা                      | She is **as** tall **as** her sister.                                                           |
| **not as/so...as**             | ততটা নয়                         | This book isn't **as** interesting **as** that one.                                             |
| **the more..., the more...**   | যত..., তত...                     | **The more** you practice, **the better** you become.                                           |
| **used to**                    | আগে করতাম                        | I **used to** play football.                                                                    |
| **be used to**                 | অভ্যস্ত হওয়া                    | I'm **used to** waking up early.                                                                |
| **get used to**                | অভ্যস্ত হয়ে যাওয়া              | You'll **get used to** the weather.                                                             |
| **would rather**               | বরং...করব                        | I'd **rather** stay home.                                                                       |
| **would prefer**               | বেশি পছন্দ করব                   | I'd **prefer** tea.                                                                             |
| **not only...but also**        | শুধু নয়, বরং                    | She is **not only** smart **but also** kind.                                                    |
| **both...and**                 | উভয়ই                            | **Both** Ali **and** Hasan came.                                                                |
| **either...or**                | হয়...নয়তো                      | **Either** call me **or** text me.                                                              |
| **neither...nor**              | কোনোটাই নয়                      | **Neither** he **nor** his brother was there.                                                   |
| **as if / as though**          | যেন                              | He talks **as if** he knows everything.                                                         |
| **by the time**                | যখন পর্যন্ত                      | **By the time** I arrived, they had left.                                                       |
| **as soon as**                 | সাথে সাথে                        | Call me **as soon as** you arrive.                                                              |
| **before**                     | আগে                              | Finish your homework **before** dinner.                                                         |
| **after**                      | পরে                              | We went home **after** the meeting.                                                             |
| **while**                      | যখন / কিন্তু                     | Don't talk **while** I'm driving.                                                               |
| **whereas**                    | অথচ / কিন্তু                     | He likes tea, **whereas** I prefer coffee.                                                      |
| **however**                    | তবে / কিন্তু                     | It was expensive. **However**, I bought it.                                                     |
| **therefore**                  | তাই                              | He studied hard; **therefore**, he passed.                                                      |
| **moreover**                   | তাছাড়া                          | She's talented. **Moreover**, she's hardworking.                                                |
| **otherwise**                  | না হলে                           | Hurry up, **otherwise** you'll miss the bus.                                                    |
| **as far as I know**           | যতদূর জানি                       | **As far as I know**, he's in Dhaka.                                                            |
| **to be honest**               | সত্যি বলতে                       | **To be honest**, I didn't like it.                                                             |
| **in fact**                    | আসলে                             | **In fact**, he's younger than me.                                                              |
| **at least**                   | অন্তত                            | **At least** try once.                                                                          |
| **after all**                  | শেষ পর্যন্ত / যাই হোক            | Don't be angry; **after all**, he's your friend.                                                |
| **on the other hand**          | অন্যদিকে                         | This phone is cheap. **On the other hand**, it's slow.                                          |
| **for example / for instance** | উদাহরণস্বরূপ                     | Many animals, **for example**, tigers, are endangered.                                          |
